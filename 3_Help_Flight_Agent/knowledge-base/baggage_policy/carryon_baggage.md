# Carry-On Baggage Policy — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines allows passengers to bring carry-on baggage on board, subject to size, weight, and quantity restrictions to ensure safety and comfort for all travelers.

---

## General Allowance
- **Economy Class:** 1 piece, max 7 kg (15 lbs)  
- **Premium Economy:** 1 piece, max 10 kg (22 lbs)  
- **Business Class:** 2 pieces, total max 15 kg (33 lbs)  
- **First Class:** 2 pieces, total max 18 kg (40 lbs)  

---

## Size Restrictions
- Maximum dimensions per piece: 55 cm × 35 cm × 25 cm (22 × 14 × 10 in)  
- Personal item (laptop bag, handbag, or briefcase) allowed in addition to main carry-on.  

---

## Prohibited Items
- Sharp objects (knives, scissors exceeding airline limits)  
- Explosives, flammable liquids, or hazardous chemicals  
- Certain sporting equipment (bats, golf clubs, etc.) unless approved in advance  
- Any items restricted by local aviation authorities  

---

## Additional Notes
- Passengers exceeding weight or size limits may need to check their carry-on baggage and pay applicable fees.  
- All carry-on items must fit in the overhead bin or under the seat in front of the passenger.  
- Special items (musical instruments, medical equipment) may have separate handling policies.
