SkyVista Airlines provides checked baggage allowances based on fare class and route. Policies aim to balance passenger convenience with operational efficiency.

---

## Allowances by Fare Class
- **Economy Class:** 20 kg (44 lbs)  
- **Premium Economy:** 25 kg (55 lbs)  
- **Business Class:** 35 kg (77 lbs)  
- **First Class:** 40 kg (88 lbs)  

> Note: Allowance may vary on specific routes; passengers are encouraged to check airline notifications before travel.

---

## Dimensions
- Maximum linear dimensions per bag (length + width + height): 158 cm (62 in)  
- Oversized bags may incur additional fees.

---

## Baggage Handling
- Checked baggage is tagged at check-in and transported in the cargo hold.  
- Passengers are responsible for verifying baggage claims upon arrival.  
- Lost or damaged baggage claims must be reported to SkyVista staff within 24 hours of arrival.  

---

## Special Items
- Sporting equipment, musical instruments, and fragile items may have separate handling requirements.  
- Passengers are advised to declare special items at check-in to ensure proper handling.
