# Oversize and Restricted Items — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines maintains specific guidelines for oversized, overweight, and restricted items to ensure safety and compliance with aviation regulations.

---

## Oversize Baggage
- Any checked bag exceeding 158 cm (62 in) linear dimensions.  
- Fees: Charged based on weight and size category; pre-paid rates available online.  
- Examples: Large suitcases, golf bags, musical instruments.

---

## Overweight Baggage
- Economy: 21–32 kg (46–70 lbs)  
- Premium Economy: 26–32 kg (57–70 lbs)  
- Business: 36–45 kg (79–99 lbs)  
- First Class: 41–50 kg (90–110 lbs)  
- Fees applied per kg over allowance.

---

## Restricted Items
- **Prohibited:** Explosives, flammable liquids, toxic substances, compressed gases.  
- **Restricted with Approval:** Batteries, e-cigarettes, sporting equipment, medical devices.  
- Items must comply with local aviation authority regulations and airline safety standards.

---

## Handling Guidelines
- Declare oversized or restricted items at check-in.  
- Proper packaging required for fragile or sensitive items.  
- Special assistance available for handling musical instruments, medical equipment, and other delicate cargo.  

SkyVista Airlines prioritizes passenger safety and compliance while providing flexible options for transporting unique or oversized items.
