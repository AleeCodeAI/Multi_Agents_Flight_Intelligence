For passengers who prefer in-person services or require assistance, SkyVista Airlines provides airport check-in counters at all operational hubs and major airports.

---

## Check-In Counters
- Open **3 hours** prior to international flights.  
- Open **2 hours** prior to domestic flights.  
- Staff available to assist with baggage drop, seat selection, and travel documentation verification.

---

## Special Assistance
- **Wheelchair Services:** Dedicated counters and priority assistance.  
- **Unaccompanied Minors (UMNR):** Supervised check-in and boarding process.  
- **Medical Assistance:** Passengers requiring medical support can check in at designated counters.

---

## Required Documents
- Valid government-issued ID or passport.  
- Flight ticket or booking reference.  
- Visas or travel authorizations if required for international destinations.

---

## Counter Services
- Baggage drop for checked luggage.  
- Issuance of printed boarding passes if not obtained online.  
- Assistance with seat changes, upgrades, and special meal requests.
