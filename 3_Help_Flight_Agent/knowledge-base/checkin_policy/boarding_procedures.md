# Boarding Procedures — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines implements a structured boarding process to ensure safety, efficiency, and on-time departures.

---

## Boarding Groups
- **Group 1:** First Class and Business Class passengers  
- **Group 2:** Premium Economy passengers  
- **Group 3:** Economy passengers by seat rows (rear to front)  
- **Special Assistance:** Passengers requiring additional support board first

---

## Gate Procedures
- Present boarding pass (digital or printed) and valid ID to gate staff.  
- Security checks must be completed prior to arriving at the gate.  
- Follow gate announcements for updated boarding instructions or delays.

---

## Priority Boarding
- SkyVista Rewards elite members receive priority boarding privileges.  
- Families with young children or passengers traveling with infants are allowed to board before general Economy groups.

---

## Safety Reminders
- All carry-on baggage must fit in the overhead bin or under the seat.  
- Electronic devices should be set to airplane mode during boarding.  
- Follow crew instructions for orderly boarding and seating.

---

SkyVista Airlines’ boarding procedures are designed to ensure a smooth, safe, and efficient start to every flight while accommodating passengers with special needs and premium services.
