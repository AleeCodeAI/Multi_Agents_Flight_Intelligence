SkyVista Airlines offers convenient online check-in options to streamline the boarding process and reduce waiting times at the airport.

---

## Availability
- Opens **48 hours** before scheduled departure for all flights.  
- Closes **2 hours** before departure for domestic flights and **3 hours** for international flights.

---

## How to Check-In
- **Website:** Visit the SkyVista Airlines official website and enter booking reference and passenger details.  
- **Mobile App:** Use the SkyVista Airlines app to check in and download or receive a digital boarding pass.  
- **Self-Service Kiosks:** Some airports offer online check-in via kiosks for additional convenience.

---

## Benefits
- Choose preferred seats (subject to availability).  
- Receive boarding passes electronically for mobile devices or print at home.  
- Save time at the airport by bypassing standard check-in counters for baggage drop.

---

## Notes
- Passengers with special assistance, unaccompanied minors, or oversized baggage must visit the airport check-in counter.  
- Ensure all travel documents and visas (if required) are valid before checking in online.  
- Mobile boarding passes are compatible with most airport security and gate scanners.
