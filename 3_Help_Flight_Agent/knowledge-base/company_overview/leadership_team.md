# Leadership Team — SkyVista Airlines

## Executive Leadership

- **Chief Executive Officer (CEO):** Ali Sina Ghulami  
  Oversees all corporate strategy, operational priorities, and long-term growth initiatives. Leads the board in decision-making, business development, and strategic partnerships.

- **Chief Operations Officer (COO):** Sarah K. Mirza  
  Responsible for day-to-day airline operations, including ground services, flight operations, and overall operational efficiency. Ensures compliance with safety and regulatory standards.

- **Chief Technology Officer (CTO):** Lina Taghavi  
  Leads the airline's digital transformation, IT infrastructure, automation systems, and AI-driven customer support solutions. Focuses on innovation and technological excellence.

- **Chief Customer Experience Officer (CCEO):** Julian Ortega  
  Oversees passenger experience, loyalty programs, and service quality. Implements customer-centric initiatives across all touchpoints.

- **Chief Financial Officer (CFO):** Farid Khan  
  Manages financial strategy, budgeting, reporting, and risk management. Ensures sustainable growth and efficient allocation of resources.

- **Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO):** Amina Reza  
  Directs talent acquisition, employee training, and organizational culture initiatives. Maintains workforce satisfaction and performance standards.

## Key Leadership Responsibilities
- Align corporate strategy with operational execution.  
- Maintain safety, compliance, and regulatory standards.  
- Oversee fleet modernization and technological innovation.  
- Monitor financial health and resource optimization.  
- Enhance customer satisfaction and brand reputation.  
- Develop workforce policies, training, and career growth programs.  

---

## Leadership Philosophy
SkyVista Airlines’ leadership team emphasizes transparency, collaboration, innovation, and accountability. The executives work collectively to ensure passenger satisfaction, operational excellence, and sustainable growth, while nurturing a culture of continuous improvement and professional development across the company.
