# Aircraft Overview — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines operates a modern and efficient fleet designed to balance regional connectivity with passenger comfort and operational reliability. All aircraft are maintained under strict safety and regulatory standards.

---

## Fleet Composition

- **Airbus A320neo**  
  - Quantity: 12  
  - Typical Seating: 180 (Economy: 150, Business: 30)  
  - Range: 6,300 km  
  - Features: Wi-Fi available, personal entertainment screens in Business class  

- **Boeing 737 MAX 8**  
  - Quantity: 10  
  - Typical Seating: 175 (Economy: 145, Business: 30)  
  - Range: 6,100 km  
  - Features: Fuel-efficient engines, ergonomic seating, Wi-Fi available  

- **Boeing 787-9 Dreamliner**  
  - Quantity: 6  
  - Typical Seating: 280 (Economy: 210, Business: 60, First: 10)  
  - Range: 14,140 km  
  - Features: Advanced cabin pressurization, larger windows, premium in-flight entertainment  

- **Airbus A350-900**  
  - Quantity: 6  
  - Typical Seating: 300 (Economy: 220, Business: 70, First: 10)  
  - Range: 15,000 km  
  - Features: Modern cabin, mood lighting, high-speed Wi-Fi, quiet engines  

---

## Fleet Highlights

- Total Aircraft: 34  
- Average Fleet Age: ~5.2 years  
- Mix of narrow-body and wide-body aircraft for regional and long-haul operations  
- Advanced navigation and safety systems installed across all aircraft  
- Regularly upgraded cabin interiors for passenger comfort  

---

## Maintenance and Safety

- Aircraft are maintained according to manufacturer guidelines and international aviation safety standards.  
- All major checks performed at certified MRO facilities.  
- Fleet management includes predictive maintenance using digital monitoring systems.  
- Safety audits conducted quarterly to ensure compliance and operational reliability.  

---

## Summary

SkyVista Airlines’ fleet is designed to provide efficient, safe, and comfortable travel experiences. By combining fuel-efficient narrow-body jets for short-haul routes and wide-body aircraft for long-haul connectivity, the airline ensures flexibility, operational excellence, and passenger satisfaction across its network.
