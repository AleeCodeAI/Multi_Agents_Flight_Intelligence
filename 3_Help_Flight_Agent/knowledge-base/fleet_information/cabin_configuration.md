# Cabin Configuration — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines provides a variety of cabin classes across its fleet to meet the needs of business, leisure, and premium travelers. Each cabin is designed to maximize comfort, convenience, and accessibility.

---

## Economy Class
- **Seating Layout:** 3-3 (narrow-body), 3-4-3 (wide-body)  
- **Seat Pitch:** 31–33 inches  
- **Seat Width:** 17–18 inches  
- **Amenities:**  
  - Personal entertainment screen on select aircraft  
  - USB and power outlets  
  - Complimentary snacks and beverages on all flights  
  - Optional buy-onboard meals for long-haul flights  

---

## Premium Economy
- **Seating Layout:** 2-3 (narrow-body), 2-4-2 (wide-body)  
- **Seat Pitch:** 37–39 inches  
- **Seat Width:** 18–19 inches  
- **Amenities:**  
  - Extra legroom and recline  
  - Priority boarding  
  - Enhanced meal service  
  - Premium entertainment options  

---

## Business Class
- **Seating Layout:** 2-2 (narrow-body), 1-2-1 (wide-body)  
- **Seat Pitch:** 55–60 inches  
- **Seat Width:** 20–22 inches  
- **Amenities:**  
  - Lie-flat or angled-flat seats on long-haul aircraft  
  - Priority check-in and boarding  
  - Premium meals with complimentary beverages  
  - Personal in-flight entertainment with noise-cancelling headphones  
  - Access to airport lounges  

---

## First Class (Available on Long-Haul Aircraft Only)
- **Seating Layout:** 1-2-1  
- **Seat Pitch:** Fully lie-flat, private suites on select aircraft  
- **Amenities:**  
  - Personal suite with sliding doors for privacy  
  - Fine dining options with curated beverages  
  - Concierge-style service from cabin crew  
  - Premium amenity kits  
  - Exclusive lounge access and fast-track airport services  

---

## Cabin Highlights
- All aircraft cabins equipped with modern lighting and climate control.  
- High-speed Wi-Fi available on select aircraft across all classes.  
- Universal power outlets and USB ports available in all classes.  
- Focus on ergonomics and comfort to ensure passenger satisfaction on both short- and long-haul flights.  

---

SkyVista Airlines’ cabin configurations are designed to balance comfort, efficiency, and service quality, ensuring a consistent and enjoyable experience for all passengers.
