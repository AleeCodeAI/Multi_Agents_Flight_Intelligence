# Inflight Services — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines offers a comprehensive range of inflight services designed to enhance passenger comfort, convenience, and overall travel experience. Services vary slightly depending on aircraft type and flight duration.

---

## Entertainment Services
- **Personal Entertainment Screens:** Available in Business and First class; select Economy and Premium Economy seats on long-haul aircraft.  
- **Content Options:** Movies, TV shows, music, podcasts, and games.  
- **In-Flight Magazines:** SkyVista monthly magazine with travel tips, news, and lifestyle features.  
- **Wi-Fi Access:** Available on all wide-body aircraft and select narrow-body aircraft. High-speed connectivity for browsing, messaging, and streaming.  

---

## Meal and Beverage Services
- **Economy Class:** Complimentary snacks and beverages; buy-onboard meal options for long-haul flights.  
- **Premium Economy:** Enhanced meal options with complimentary beverages, including soft drinks and select alcoholic beverages.  
- **Business Class:** Multi-course meals with complimentary beverages, including wines, spirits, and specialty coffees/teas.  
- **First Class:** Gourmet dining with personalized menus, premium wines, champagne, and chef-curated options.  

---

## Special Services
- **Special Meals:** Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, kosher, halal, and child-friendly meals available upon request.  
- **Medical Assistance:** Crew trained to assist with minor medical emergencies; advanced support coordinated with ground medical teams.  
- **Unaccompanied Minors (UMNR):** Dedicated cabin crew supervision and support throughout the flight.  
- **Wheelchair and Mobility Assistance:** Available for boarding, deplaning, and onboard mobility needs.  

---

## Comfort Amenities
- **Blankets and Pillows:** Provided in Premium Economy, Business, and First Class; available on request in Economy.  
- **Amenity Kits:** Offered in Business and First Class with toiletries, eye masks, earplugs, and other essentials.  
- **Noise-Cancelling Headphones:** Provided in Business and First Class; optional purchase in Premium Economy and Economy.  

---

## Customer Support
- Cabin crew available 24/7 during flights to address passenger inquiries, requests, and concerns.  
- Real-time support through in-flight entertainment system for information on connecting flights, baggage, and loyalty programs.  
- Feedback collection to continuously improve service quality.  

---

SkyVista Airlines’ inflight services focus on creating a comfortable, enjoyable, and seamless journey for all passengers, combining modern technology, high-quality meals, and attentive crew support.
