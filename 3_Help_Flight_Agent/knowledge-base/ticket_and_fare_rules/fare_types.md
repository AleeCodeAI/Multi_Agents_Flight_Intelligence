SkyVista Airlines offers a variety of fare types to cater to different passenger needs, travel flexibility, and budget preferences.

---

## Economy Class Fares
- **Saver:** Lowest fare, non-refundable, limited changes, basic seat selection.  
- **Standard:** Moderate fare, refundable with a fee, seat selection included, some flexibility in changes.  
- **Flex:** Higher fare, fully refundable, priority boarding, and free seat changes.

---

## Premium Economy Fares
- **Standard:** Flexible fare with moderate refund and change policies, enhanced meal service, extra legroom.  
- **Flex:** Fully refundable, priority services, and access to select lounge facilities.

---

## Business Class Fares
- **Business Saver:** Reduced fare for advance booking, partially refundable, standard services.  
- **Business Flex:** Fully refundable, priority boarding, lounge access, and flexible itinerary changes.

---

## First Class Fares
- **First Saver:** Limited availability, partially refundable, premium cabin services.  
- **First Flex:** Fully refundable, priority services, personal suite, and concierge support.

---

## Additional Notes
- Fare rules may vary depending on route and season.  
- Loyalty program members may have additional fare options and upgrades.  
- Discounts available for children, infants, and group bookings.
