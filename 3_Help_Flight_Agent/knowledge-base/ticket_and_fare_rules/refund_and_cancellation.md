# Refund and Cancellation — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines provides clear guidelines for refunds and cancellations to ensure transparency and passenger convenience.

---

## Cancellation Policies
- **Economy Saver:** Non-refundable; cancellation not permitted.  
- **Economy Standard & Flex:** Refundable with applicable fee; fee varies by fare type.  
- **Premium Economy, Business, and First Class:** Refundable based on fare rules; higher flexibility for Flex fares.

---

## Refund Process
- Requests can be submitted via the SkyVista Airlines website, mobile app, or customer service center.  
- Refunds processed to the original form of payment within 7–14 business days.  
- Partial refunds may apply for partially used tickets or change fees.

---

## Special Cases
- **Flight Cancellations by Airline:** Full refund or alternative flight offered at no cost.  
- **Medical Emergencies:** Refunds or rebooking considered with valid documentation.  
- **Government Travel Restrictions:** Refunds or travel credits issued for applicable cases.

---

## Notes
- Travel insurance may cover additional costs not reimbursed by the airline.  
- Refund eligibility depends on fare type, ticket status, and applicable terms and conditions.
