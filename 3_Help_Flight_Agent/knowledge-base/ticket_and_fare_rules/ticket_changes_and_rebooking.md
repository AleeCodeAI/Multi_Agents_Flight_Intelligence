# Ticket Changes and Rebooking — SkyVista Airlines

SkyVista Airlines allows passengers to modify bookings according to fare rules, providing flexibility for travel plans.

---

## Change Policies
- **Economy Saver:** Changes not permitted.  
- **Economy Standard & Flex:** Changes allowed with applicable fees; fare difference may apply.  
- **Premium Economy, Business, and First Class:** Flexible changes, fees vary by fare type; Flex fares often include free changes.

---

## How to Change a Ticket
- **Online:** Use SkyVista Airlines website or mobile app to manage booking.  
- **Customer Service:** Contact the call center or support email for assistance.  
- **Airport Counter:** Changes accepted at check-in counters, subject to availability.

---

## Rebooking Rules
- New travel dates and times subject to seat availability.  
- Fare difference may apply if the new flight is priced higher.  
- Changes must be completed before original flight departure to avoid no-show penalties.

---

## Special Considerations
- Loyalty program members may receive waived change fees or additional flexibility.  
- Group bookings and corporate fares may have separate change policies.  
- Passengers affected by airline cancellations are rebooked at no additional charge.

---

SkyVista Airlines’ ticket change and rebooking policies aim to balance passenger flexibility with operational requirements, ensuring a smooth experience for travelers needing schedule adjustments.
